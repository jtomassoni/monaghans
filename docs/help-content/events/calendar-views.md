# Calendar Views - Navigating Week and Month Views

View all scheduled content—events, specials, and announcements—in one place.

## Calendar Views

Two viewing modes:

### Week View
- Current week (Sunday–Saturday)
- Hourly time slots
- Best for detailed scheduling
- Click **"Week"** in the toolbar

### Month View
- Entire month overview
- Events shown as date blocks
- Best for planning ahead
- Click **"Month"** in the toolbar

## Navigation

- **◀ Previous**: Previous week/month
- **▶ Next**: Next week/month
- **Today**: Jump to current date

## Content Types

- **Events**: Title and time
- **Specials**: Food and drink deals
- **Announcements**: Active announcements

## Quick Actions

### Create from Calendar
1. Click a date
2. Choose: Event, Food Special, Drink Special, or Announcement
3. Fill details and save

### Reschedule Events
1. Click and hold an event
2. Drag to new date
3. Release to update

**Note**: Dragging recurring events creates an exception for that occurrence only.

## Display Features

- Business hours highlighted
- Color-coded by type
- All-day events at top of day

## Tips

- **Week View**: For time-specific scheduling
- **Month View**: For overview and planning
- Click any event to view/edit
- Use search/filter to find events
