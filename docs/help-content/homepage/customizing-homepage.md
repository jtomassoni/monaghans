# Customizing Your Homepage

First thing customers see. Customize hero section, about content, and layout.

## Access Settings

1. Go to [Settings](/admin/settings)
2. Find **Homepage** section
3. Customize options

## Hero Section

Large banner at top of homepage.

### Customize Hero

1. In Settings → Homepage, find **Hero Section**
2. Customize:
   - **Hero Image**: Upload/select background image
   - **Hero Text**: Headline (if supported)
   - **Hero Subtext**: Supporting text (if supported)

### Hero Image Tips

- Use high-quality, high-resolution images
- At least 1920px wide
- Consider text overlay when choosing
- Keep file sizes reasonable for fast loading

## About Section

Information about your establishment.

### Update About Content

1. In Settings → Homepage, find **About Section**
2. Update:
   - **About Text**: Business description
   - **About Image**: Optional image
3. Save

### About Content Tips

- Concise but informative
- Highlight what makes you unique
- Include key info: hours, location, specialties
- Update regularly to keep fresh

## Homepage Images

Upload and manage images:

1. In Settings → Homepage, find **Images** or **Gallery**
2. Upload new images
3. Reorder or remove existing
4. Images used in:
   - Hero section
   - About section
   - Image carousel (if enabled)

## Homepage Layout

Typically includes:
1. **Hero Section**: Large banner at top
2. **Announcements**: Time-sensitive announcements
3. **About Section**: Business information
4. **Events/Specials**: Upcoming events and specials
5. **Image Gallery**: Establishment photos (if enabled)

## Content Display

Homepage automatically shows:
- **Active Announcements**: Published within date range
- **Upcoming Events**: Scheduled for near future
- **Current Specials**: Active food and drink specials
- **Business Hours**: Current hours

## Tips

- Keep hero images and about content fresh
- Ensure mobile-friendly display
- Optimize images for fast loading
- Make value proposition clear
- Include clear calls-to-action
