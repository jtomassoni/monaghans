# Creating and Managing Food Specials

Promote daily or weekly food deals.

## Create Special

1. Go to [Food Specials](/admin/food-specials)
2. Click **"New Food Special"**
3. Fill details and save

## Special Details

### Required
- **Title**: Special name (e.g., "Taco Tuesday")
- **Type**: "Food" (auto-set)

### Optional
- **Description**: Special details
- **Price Notes**: Pricing (e.g., "$14.00", "Half Price")
- **Weekday Selection**: Days of week
- **Time Window**: Time range (e.g., "4pm-6pm", "All Day")
- **Date Range**: Start/end dates
- **Image**: Upload special image
- **Status**: Active (visible) or Inactive (hidden)

## Weekday-Based Specials

Specials that repeat weekly:

1. Check days when special applies:
   - Monday through Sunday
2. Special appears on those days each week

**Example**: "Taco Tuesday" with Tuesday checked appears every Tuesday.

## Date Range Specials

Specials for specific date ranges:

1. Set **Start Date**
2. Optionally set **End Date**
3. Special appears on all dates in range

**Example**: "Holiday Special" Dec 1-31:
- Start: December 1
- End: December 31

## Time Windows

When during the day the special applies:

Enter time range in **Time Window** field:
- "4pm-6pm" (Happy Hour)
- "11am-2pm" (Lunch)
- "All Day"
- "After 8pm"

## Upload Images

1. Click **Image** button in form
2. Select image from computer or gallery
3. Image displays on calendar and homepage

**Tip**: Use high-quality images that showcase the food.

## Edit Special

1. Go to [Food Specials](/admin/food-specials)
2. Click the special
3. Update details
4. Save

## Delete Special

1. Go to [Food Specials](/admin/food-specials)
2. Click delete icon (trash)
3. Confirm

## Status

- **Active**: Visible on calendar and homepage
- **Inactive**: Hidden (not deleted)

Use inactive to temporarily hide without deleting.

## Display Locations

Food specials appear on:
- **Calendar** (scheduled dates)
- **Homepage** (when active)
- **Specials Gallery** (if enabled)

## Tips

- Use clear, recognizable titles
- Keep pricing current
- Use high-quality images
- Plan ahead for week/month
- Use consistent time windows
